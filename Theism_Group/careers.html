Learning & Development

At RPSG Group, L&D programmes are aimed at nurturing people for a future-ready organisation through an intellectually stimulating and challenging environment.

Leadership Development Programs

LDP is an intensive in-house programme, customised for high performing managers. It blends experiential learning and conceptual inputs with opportunities of self-introspection and experimentation. This enables a smooth transition from leading functions to managing and leading teams across disciplines and businesses.

Development Centre

DC is a structured method of objectively mapping the competencies of our people and defining their future growth plans. It helps to identify high-fliers to support their growth and development as leaders.

Conclaves

Providing ample opportunities of sharing best practices and knowledge, the Conclaves have various modules that can help in peer-to-peer connect and enhance functional collaboration between our Group companies. It helps in fostering inter-company synergy and provides dedicated opportunities to get innovative solutions.
